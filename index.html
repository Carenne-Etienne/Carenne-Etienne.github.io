My Name is Astera Garnet and I am a self-taught digital artist! I first started in art as a kid doing traditional art and sketching both at home and at school. When I entered middle school I was encouraged by my art teachers to engage in art a lot more to learn and discover new and different mediums. I explored painting, sculpting, and crafting until I came across digital art in high school. Now I do digital art on the side while I finish college and hope to continue to grow and learn in the craft as time goes on! I hope you join me for the ride as I continue to grow and learn!
